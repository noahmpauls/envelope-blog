Font Styles

Bold

There are many different styles of fonts. One such style is the bold style of font. Bold is used to display emphasis, sometimes for a single word, or perhaps for an entire phrase within a sentence. The way it is used is often inconsistent. It can be infuriating to read improperly bolded text.

Italics

Instead, you might end up relying on italics. Italics also convey emphasis, but of a different sort than bold letters. Bold letters imply an increase in volume, while italics imply a change in tone. At least, that's how a inexperienced reader like Bob might see it.

Combination

A combination of bold and italic might be used by someone who really demands attention. Or they might just want to show a ramp-up in emphasis.

Lists

Ordered Lists

Sometimes, I just want to put down some things in order. A list of steps for writing this blog, perhaps. How about it?

  1. Start by thinking of a topic you want to write about. In this case, I chose to write a whole set of prose about literally nothing. How exciting!

  2. Begin writing. Lift up your pencil, and write out a bunch of text on your computer screen.

  3. Realize that you've made a terrible mistake:

    1. Yeah, this is definitely a problem. What were you thinking writing on your computer screen?
    2. Don't panic. It will come off.
    3. It's not coming off.
    4. Oof.

  4. Continue the creative process by throwing you computer out the window.

  5. You're done!

That was so fun! Writing lists makes me so excited. But sometimes my excitement has more disorder than order. That's when I reach for my other tool...

Unordered Lists

Lists without law and order are bound to get into serious trouble. Just look at this one:

  • I'm a list item that likes to litter.

  • Littering's nothing. I sell those illegal fireworks, and I'm not even worry about it.

  • Oh yeah? Well I'm planning to rob a bank. Here are a few of the things I hope to find inside:

    • Those arcade game tickets where you have to have approximately a billion to get one of the really cool prizes so you usually just settle for something lame like a candy bar
    • A stuffed crust pizza

  • I make terrible puns. For instance, have you ever thought about how our sun is always guilty of our-sun?

  • I'm planning to murder the pun-making list item above me for the good of all.

As you can see, unordered lists aren't for the faint of heart.

Block Quotes

Block quotes are for when you don't have anything inspired to say yourself, so instead you farm out what you want to say to someone smarter than you.

The efficacious advantage of posterity manifests in the form of eccentric flibblegabble, a roundabout of which will make any democratically-minded crustacean wince within the confines of his ethereal locale.

Whether you talk or you grok, there are no shimmies in the quest for statehood. Regular oil changes will aid in the quest to become a regular Socrates. How do you like them peaches?

See how much page space that filled? Now imagine just using block quotes for everything. You'd never have to write originally ever again!

Very smart. Very smart indeed.
